Talk to a strategist

Do I need to record videos?

+
Yes, but we show you exactly how. We send you easy-to-follow guides on what to say and how to film. Then we edit it into professional content.

How many times will you post?

+
We usually post 4 to 6 high-quality Reels or Shorts per week, plus daily Stories to keep your brand at the top of people's feeds.

What platforms do you manage?

+
We handle Instagram,LinkedIn, YouTube, and X. We focus on the ones where your customers actually hang out.

Do you reply to comments?

+
Yes. Our team monitors your comments and DMs to answer customer questions, hide spam, and keep the community positive.

Will I see the posts before they go live?

+
Always. We use a simple calendar where you can see all upcoming posts and approve or suggest changes before anything is published.

Do you help with influencers?

+
Yes. We find the right people for your brand, talk to them, and manage the whole collaboration from start to finish.

How long is a typical contract?

+
We usually work in 3-month cycles. This gives the social media algorithms enough time to learn and start showing your content to more people.

Is my account secure?

+
Yes. We use professional security tools to log into your accounts, making sure your brand assets are always protected and safe.

How do you track success?

+
We focus on how many people are actually talking about your brand and visiting your website, not just empty numbers like "likes."

Can I manage it myself later?

+
Of course. We set up everything so it's clean and organized. If you ever want to take over, we'll hand over all the materials and access smoothly.
