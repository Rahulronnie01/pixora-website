Pixora was founded in 2021 by a team of ex-FAANG engineers and award-winning creative directors who saw a massive disconnect between traditional "boutique" marketing agencies and the high-tech reality of modern digital platforms.

We spent the first twelve months building a proprietary data engine that allowed us to analyze millions of ad interactions. This knowledge base became the foundation of what Pixora is today: an agency that operates with the speed of a startup and the precision of a software company.
