Audit your funnel

What is A/B testing?

+
It's simple: we show two versions of a page to see which one makes more people buy. The winner becomes your new permanent page.

How long does a test run?

+
Usually 7 to 14 days. We wait until we have enough data to be 100% sure the new version is better before we switch it.

Do I need lots of traffic?

+
Ideally, at least 5,000 visitors a month. If you have less, we focus on fixing obvious design issues first to help you grow.

Will it slow my site down?

+
Not at all. We use very small code that doesn't affect your site speed. Your visitors won't even notice the testing tools.

Do you do the design?

+
Yes. Our team designs and builds all the new variations. We make sure everything perfectly matches your brand's look and feel.

What tools do you use?

+
We use professional tools like Hotjar and VWO to see how people move on your site and where they get stuck.

Do you guarantee results?

+
We guarantee to find ways to improve your sales. Our average client sees a 15% to 30% jump in efficiency within the first year.

Is this for e-commerce?

+
Yes, but not only. We optimize any site that has a goal, whether it's selling products or getting new leads.

Can you fix my checkout?

+
Absolutely. Fixing the checkout is the fastest way to make more money. We simplify forms and add trust markers to stop people from leaving.

How do you find the problems?

+
We look for things that confuse or distract your users. Combining our expertise with real data shows us exactly what to fix.
