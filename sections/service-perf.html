What is a good return on ads?

+
Every business is different, but we usually aim for $3 back for every $1 spent. We focus on your actual profit, not just "likes" or "clicks."

Who owns the accounts?

+
You do. You keep 100% ownership of your accounts and data. We just link our partner account to manage the ads for you.

What is the minimum budget?

+
We recommend starting with at least $3,000 per month. This allows us to test enough ads to find the winners that really drive sales.

How soon will I see sales?

+
You'll see data immediately. Usually, it takes 14 to 21 days for us to find the "winning" ads that we can really scale up for big results.

Do you make the videos?

+
Yes. We help you create and edit videos that are designed specifically to stop the scroll and get people to buy your product.

How do you track sales?

+
We use modern tracking tools to see exactly where your customers come from, so we know which ads are making you money.

Will I have a contact person?

+
Yes. You'll have a dedicated person you can reach on Slack any time for daily updates and questions.

Do you work on commission?

+
For some brands, we can work on a performance basis where we only get paid more when you grow more.

Which platforms do you use?

+
We manage ads across Facebook, Instagram, Google, and YouTube to make sure your brand is seen everywhere it matters.

How long do clients stay?

+
On average, our clients stay with us for over 14 months because we focus on long-term growth, not just one-off campaigns.
